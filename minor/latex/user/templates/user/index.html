About Us

This is a great tool to create documents. It's based on the 'WYSIWYM' (what you see is what you mean) idea, meaning you only have to focus on the contents of your document and the computer will take care of the formatting. With this kind of environment, it's very easy to create professional-looking material.

Our Project

The very idea of our project is derived from ShareLaTex which is a popular website providing similar services to its user.
